Visualizations


1) University Dataset: SAT score averages of Females and Males

This visualization shows the SAT average scores between the Females and Males students that were admitted over the years. To make it interactive, if you hover your mouse on any data point then it will also show the SAT average from the other visualization as a comparison. This helps in doing year by year analysis and also provides a complete image of the data to facilitate the discussion about process.

2) University Dataset: Ethinic Diversity accross colleges among Females and Males

The following viz helps to visualize the distribution of different ethnic groups across different universities in US.

3) Mozilla Dataset: Demographic distribution of highly ranked[1/2/3] parameters in making purchase of a tech toy

This demographic map shows the demographic distribution of high ranked parameters in purchasing of a tech toy. By moving mouse on each specific country, you can view the parameter percentage of that country. On the top of the map you can select the parameter, which shows the significance of selected parameter to each country in the world. To make the map interactive, the countries are highlighted if hovered over by mouse, on clicking any country, the map zooms in to that selected country and brings it to the center of the screen.
